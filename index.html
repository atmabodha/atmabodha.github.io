Our Mission

We are building the digital infrastructure for Vedantic studies. By leveraging Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing, we aim to uncover patterns, create accessible translations, and preserve the sanctity of these texts for future generations.

  • Universal Access: Making texts accessible via modern APIs.
  • Computational Analysis: Applying LLMs to interpret commentaries.
  • Open Collaboration: A community-driven approach to Digital Dharma.
